    It used to be a super big deal to me but now, in hindsight, it is just a joke. 

                          There once was a time where I was trying to meet everyone's expectation
                      I want to be accepted or maybe praised by people around me
                      so I was doing my best to be different things around different people
                      just to make others like me
                      Overworking myself, it is unpleasant for me to keep it up every day
                      Having much pressure to take on by myself, I was helpless but don't know what to do

                      In the end, I find my pastor for help
                      She told me that I dont need to be so worried about having to make everyone happy
                      And I am perfectly fine with who I am 
                      I wasn't so sure at first
                      Since that is what I have been doing for a long time
                      But after some time to think about it
                      I finally accept that no one can be perfect for everyone

                      Looking at it now, it is stupid for me to try to meet everyone's expection
                      I should be focusing on what I desire, I cannot befriend everyone in my life
                      I was so much better after relieving the pressure
                      I dont care about everyone's expectation now and I am performing so much better
                      So yeah I think that is it
