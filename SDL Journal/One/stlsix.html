    What do you hope to be like in 10 years’ time?

    10 years is a long time for me. I am only 16 and 10 years is already a big portion of my life. Reading this question, I have suddenly realised that I had never planned my future, I don't know what should I write when I have never in mind. I feel like 10 years is too far from now on, that it isn't in my priority to even think about it. 10 years from now, I will be 26 years old. Some may say it is a golden time for teen/youth Some may also say it is when you know you're getting old and problems start rushing in your life If I can choose, I would not grow up. 10 years from now, I wish to stay young forever. Taking up more responsibility means more stress, and I don't think I can handle this much stress. I want to see myself able to enjoy life. Maybe going around the world, maybe just staying in Hong Kong I want to see myself happy and grateful to everything in life. I don't have much desire, or even dream like others. I don't care if I have a lot of money of not.
