    A time I felt like an outsider in a group

    During the time that I hasn't been to school, it was around December. It was a day after the Christmas Service and I have arranged a BBQ trip with three of my friends. I was looking forward to it for a while and when that day came I was so excited. However, when I went down my house and met them, I realise some of the other friends of my friend also followed I was not a person who likes meeting people I don't really talk with much in my daily life. I was uncomfortable and sad, since it wasnt in my plan and no one had told me beforehand I feel like I don't belong so I just went back home But I am still very sad about that and I was very annoyed by it I don't understand why they were afraid of hurting others feeling by refusing to let them come by But doesnt give a dang that I was not happy My heart still have an ache in it and to be honest, I was still a bit mad till this day However, I dont have the gut to tell them, and I don't know if I should tell any of them
